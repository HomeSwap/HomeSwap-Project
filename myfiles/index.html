How Does It Work

You are just a few steps away from starting your adventure:
  • Find the right apartment for you according to your desired destination, date and any other characteristics.
  • Order your desired apartment and wait for the authorization of the apartment owner.
  • The apartment owner will recieve your apartment details and reviews.
  • After the apartment owner's approval, each of you will be receive an email notification confirming with the transaction details.
  • Have a pleasant swap. Go start packing :)
